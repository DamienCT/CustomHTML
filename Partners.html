The Buck Institute

The world’s first institute devoted solely to the biology of aging—and still a global pace-setter.

The Buck is an independent biomedical research institute pioneering mechanisms of aging to extend healthy years of life. Based in Novato, California, it launched its research program in 1999 and grew into a global leader with centers like the Glenn Center for Biology of Aging and the USC-Buck Nathan Shock Center, spanning senescence, metabolism, and translational trials.

Advanced Medical Learning Centre

Mauritius’ home for hands-on clinical upskilling—turning longevity science into bedside practice.

AMLC is a professional training centre in Mauritius focused on advancing clinical skills for healthcare teams—delivering accredited, practical programmes and specialty workshops. Recent initiatives include multi-day dialysis training delivered with local hospital partners, supporting the island’s capacity for high-quality chronic-care medicine.
